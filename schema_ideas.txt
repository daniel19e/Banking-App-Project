User (UserId, Name, LastName, DOB)
  Primary Key: UserId
BankAccount(AccountNumber, Balance, UserId, DateOfCreation)
  Primary Key: AccountNumber
  Foreign Key: UserId(User)
Transaction(TransactionId, TransactionType, Amount, Timestamp, AccountNumber)
  Primary Key: TransactionId
  Foreign Key: AccountNumber(BankAccount)
Report(ReportId, AccountId, UserId, Date)
  Primary Key: ReportId
  Foreign Key: AccountId
  Foreign Key: UserId

User Has BankAccount relationship is one-to-many (one user can have multiple accounts but one account cannot belong to multiple users)
BankAccount Has Transactions is a one-to-many relationship
